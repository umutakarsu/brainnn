# Neuromodulator-Conditioned Neural Decoders for BCI: A Synthesis

## Methodology
Five parallel literature-review passes were run (PubMed/CrossRef/arXiv, DOI/PMID-verified, ~2023-2026 priority): (1) neuromodulator-decoder conditioning literature, (2) physiological markers of NE/DA/ACh state, (3) BCI architectures for non-stationarity, (4) FiLM/cross-attention/adapter conditioning mechanisms, (5) pharmacological falsifiability designs. All citations below were independently verified by the sub-agents; nothing was fabricated. Where verification failed, this is flagged explicitly.

## 1. Neuromodulator-Conditioned Decoders: Does This Exist?
**Gap finding: no published BCI decoder conditions directly on inferred ACh/NE/DA state.** The Yu & Dayan (2005) framework itself is solid ground truth: ACh = expected uncertainty, NE = unexpected uncertainty [Yu & Dayan, *Neuron* 46(4):681-692, DOI:10.1016/j.neuron.2005.04.026]. Related work exists on reward/dopamine signals in motor cortex relevant to BCI reward-modulated learning [Derosiere et al. 2025, *Nat Commun* 16:1307, DOI:10.1038/s41467-024-55016-0; Marsh et al. 2015, *J Neurosci* 35(19):7374, DOI:10.1523/JNEUROSCI.1802-14.2015], and dopaminergic/DBS effects on volitional-action dynamics in Parkinson's [Köhler et al. 2024, *Brain* 147(10):3358, DOI:10.1093/brain/awae219]. **No prior work conditions a decoder's architecture on a 3-dimensional neuromodulator estimate** — this is a genuine, unclaimed niche. **Brain2Qwerty**: I could not fully independently re-verify "Brain2Qwerty v2, 2026" in this pass (session truncated before final confirmation); treat any specific architectural claims about it as unconfirmed until checked directly against Meta AI's publication page or arXiv.

## 2. Real-Time Markers of Neuromodulator State
- **NE (best-supported):** pupil diameter tracks LC-NE firing directly [Joshi, Li, Kalwani & Gold 2016, *Neuron* 89(1):221, DOI:10.1016/j.neuron.2015.11.028; Reimer et al. 2014, *Neuron* 84(2):355; Reimer et al. 2016, *Nat Commun* 7:13289]. Theoretical scaffold: adaptive gain theory [Aston-Jones & Cohen 2005, *Annu Rev Neurosci* 28:403]. Pupil-linked belief updating under unexpected uncertainty: Nassar et al. 2012, *Nat Neurosci* 15(7):1040. P300/pupil jointly index LC-NE arousal [Murphy et al. 2011, *Psychophysiology* 48(11):1532].
- **DA:** largely theoretical/indirect. Tonic-dopamine/vigor model [Niv, Daw, Joel & Dayan 2007, *Psychopharmacology* 191(3):507]; "What does dopamine mean?" [Berke 2018, *Nat Neurosci* 21(6):787]; reaction-time variability link is correlational at best (catecholamine gene studies) — **no validated real-time DA estimator exists**.
- **ACh:** precision-weighting formalized via active inference [Parr & Friston 2017, *J R Soc Interface* 14(136):20170376]; joint NE/ACh belief-updating dissociation [Jepma et al. 2018, *J Cogn Neurosci* 30(12):1803]. No standalone real-time EEG-only ACh estimator is validated — pupillometry conflates NE+ACh contributions [Reimer et al. 2016].
**Bottom line: only NE has a mature, real-time-capable, validated marker (pupillometry). DA and ACh estimation for closed-loop use remains an open problem** — this is a genuine research contribution opportunity, not just an implementation detail.

## 3. Comparison to Existing Non-Stationarity/Attention-State BCI Approaches
Dominant paradigms handle drift **implicitly and unsupervised**: manifold/latent-dynamics alignment, Riemannian geometry [Congedo, Barachant & Bhatia 2017], Euclidean alignment [He & Wu 2020], test-time adaptation [Wimpff, Döbler & Yang 2024], LFADS [Pandarinath et al. 2018, *Nat Methods*, DOI:10.1038/s41592-018-0109-9], switching LDS [Linderman & Johnson 2017]. These infer a latent state with **no built-in semantics** — "attention" or "arousal" emerges only as an unlabeled dimension requiring post-hoc interpretation. A neuromodulator-conditioned approach is differentiated by using an **interpretable, theory-grounded, externally-manipulable** conditioning signal (testable via pharmacology — see §5), rather than a black-box latent code. This is the strongest architectural/scientific selling point of the proposed direction.

## 4. Architectural Proposals
Given the existing prototype in this repo (`src/brainnn/bci/neuromod_decoder.py`), which already implements FiLM conditioning on a 3-5 dim neuromodulator vector via a zero-initialized MLP generator (γ,β) applied to CLS features:

| Mechanism | Params | Best for | Verdict for 3-scalar neuromod signal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **FiLM** [Perez et al. 2018, arXiv:1709.07871] | O(hidden×2×dim) | low-dim, continuous, global conditioning | **Best fit** — matches existing implementation, cheap, zero-init preserves base model at start |
| **Cross-attention** [Vaswani et al. 2017, arXiv:1706.03762] | O(dim²) | high-dim/sequential conditioning (text, multi-token context) | Overkill for 3 scalars; adds unnecessary capacity/compute |
| **Adapters (Houlsby/LoRA)** [Houlsby et al. 2019, arXiv:1902.00751; Hu et al. 2021, arXiv:2106.09685] | O(rank×dim) per layer | per-subject/session personalization, discrete categorical conditioning | Better suited to subject-ID conditioning than continuous neuromodulator scalars |

**Recommendation: keep FiLM as primary mechanism** (already implemented correctly — zero-init final layer, `(1+γ)` scaling for near-identity init). Optionally combine with a lightweight adapter for per-subject calibration on top of the neuromod-FiLM layer, since subject identity and neuromodulator state are orthogonal sources of variation.

## 5. Falsifiability
Genuine causal tests require pharmacological dissociation, following established double-dissociation logic [Frank, Seeberger & O'Reilly 2004, *Science* 306:1940 — L-DOPA improved positive-feedback learning but impaired negative-feedback learning, a true two-way dissociation, not generic enhancement]. Proposed experiments:
1. **NE arm:** propranolol (β-blocker, ↓NE) vs. atomoxetine (↑NE) — predict the NE-FiLM term's contribution to decoding accuracy should shrink/grow selectively, tracked via pupillometry as the ground-truth NE proxy [Joshi et al. 2016; de Gee et al. 2014, *PNAS* 111(5):E618].
2. **DA arm:** L-DOPA/haloperidol manipulation with baseline dopamine-synthesis-capacity stratification [Petzold et al. 2019, *Sci Rep* 9:5652 & 17927] — predicts selective effect on value-gated confidence, dissociable from general arousal [Vellani et al. 2020, *eLife* 9:e59152 — showed DA selectively affects info-seeking, not generic reward-seeking, a needed specificity control].
3. **Control/ablation:** shuffle the neuromodulator conditioning vector across trials (breaking the true state-signal correlation) — if decoding accuracy is unchanged, the FiLM term was not doing genuine conditioning work, only adding capacity.
